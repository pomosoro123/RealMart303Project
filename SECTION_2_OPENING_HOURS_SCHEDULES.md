## TIME FORMAT GUIDE

### Use This Format:
- ‚úÖ "7:00 AM - 2:00 PM"
- ‚úÖ "9:00 AM - 6:00 PM"
- ‚úÖ "6:00 AM - 5:00 PM"

### Do NOT Use:
- ‚ùå "7-2" (unclear)
- ‚ùå "07:00 - 14:00" (too technical)
- ‚ùå "morning to evening" (too vague)

### Special Cases:
- "CLOSED" - Market doesn't operate this day
- "24 Hours" - Open around the clock
- "By Appointment Only" - Need to call ahead
- "Seasonal" - Market only operates certain times of year
