## HOW TO ORGANIZE YOUR DATA

### Step 1: Create Your Spreadsheet
Use Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or LibreOffice Calc (all are FREE)

### Step 2: Add Column Headers
Create columns in this exact order:
1. Market Name
2. City
3. State
4. Address
5. Phone Number
6. Email
7. Website
8. Description

### Step 3: Fill in One Market Per Row
Each row = one market with all its information

### Step 4: Format for Quality
- Keep phone numbers in consistent format (with country code and dash/parentheses)
- Keep addresses complete with zip codes
- Keep descriptions professional and informative

### Step 5: Review and Verify
Go through the entire spreadsheet and check against the quality checklist above

## QUESTIONS TO GUIDE YOUR RESEARCH

As you collect data, ask yourself:

1. Is this a real, operating market?
2. Are the opening hours recent and accurate?
3. Can someone actually find this place using the address?
4. Is the contact information correct and current?
5. Would I trust this information if I was looking for a market?
